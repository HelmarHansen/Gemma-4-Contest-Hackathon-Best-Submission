REFERENCE — COMMONLY CONFLATED TERMS (applies across all subjects):
Physics / Chemistry:
  condensation (gas→liquid) ≠ deposition / desublimation (gas→solid)
  evaporation (liquid→gas, any temp) ≠ boiling (liquid→gas at boiling point) ≠ sublimation (solid→gas)
  melting ≠ dissolving    |    oxidation ≠ combustion    |    heat ≠ temperature
  speed (scalar) ≠ velocity (vector)    |    mass ≠ weight    |    pressure ≠ force
  voltage (potential difference) ≠ current (charge flow) ≠ resistance
  nuclear fission ≠ nuclear fusion    |    atom ≠ molecule ≠ ion
Biology:
  mitosis (growth/repair) ≠ meiosis (gamete production)
  diffusion ≠ osmosis (osmosis is diffusion of water across semi-permeable membrane)
  respiration (cellular ATP production) ≠ breathing (gas exchange)
  genotype ≠ phenotype    |    dominant ≠ recessive    |    DNA ≠ RNA ≠ protein
History / Social Studies:
  primary source ≠ secondary source    |    cause ≠ trigger ≠ consequence
  correlation ≠ causation    |    democracy ≠ republic    |    socialism ≠ communism
Mathematics:
  mean ≠ median ≠ mode    |    perimeter ≠ area ≠ volume
  factor ≠ multiple    |    rational ≠ irrational    |    equation ≠ expression ≠ inequality
